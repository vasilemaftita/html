WWF

The WORLD wINDE fund for Nature (WWF) is an internationa organization working on issues regarding the conservtion, research and restoration of the environment, formerly named the World Wildlife Fund. WWF was founded in 1961.

WWF's Panda symbol

The Panda has become the symbol of WWF. The well-known panda logo of WWF originated from a panda names Chi Chi that was transferred from the Beijing Zoo to the same year of the establishment of WWF.
